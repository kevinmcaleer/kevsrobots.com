## Ahoy there makers!

I've got something cool to share with you today - a brand new **public stats dashboard** for KevsRobots.com!

You know what I love about the maker community? **Transparency**. We share our code, our designs, our failures, and our successes. So why not share the website analytics too?

## What's the Stats Dashboard?

The [new stats dashboard](/stats) is a completely transparent view into what's happening on KevsRobots.com. No login required, no paywalls - just pure data about what makers like you are searching for, viewing, and enjoying.

## Why Did I Build This?

Honestly? **Because it's useful for everyone.**

### For You (The Maker)

- **Discover trending topics** you might want to learn about
- **Find popular projects** that other makers love
- **See what the community is interested in** right now
- **Validate your project ideas** by checking if others are searching for similar topics

### For Me (Content Creator)

- **Understand what tutorials you actually want**
- **Spot gaps** in the content I'm providing
- **See which projects resonate** with the community
- **Make data-driven decisions** about what to create next

It's a win-win!

- **DuckDB Analytics** - Open-source analytics
- **Chart.js** - Beautiful, responsive charts
- **Hourly Updates** - Fresh data every 60 minutes
