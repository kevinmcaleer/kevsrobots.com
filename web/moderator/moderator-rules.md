# Moderator Rules

By being a Moderator, you agree to abide by our simple 10 rules:

1. Be respectful and fair when interacting with viewers.
2. Remove any comments that contain hate speech, harassment, or spam.
3. Promote a positive and welcoming environment in the chat.
4. Follow the channel's guidelines and enforce them consistently.
5. Avoid engaging in arguments or conflicts in the chat.
6. Notify the channel owner of any recurring issues or problematic users.
7. Use moderation tools responsibly and avoid abusing your privileges.
8. Be a role model for good behaviour on the stream.
9. Keep your own comments family-friendly (my Mum, Aunt and Daughter watch this stream too!)
10. Have fun, encourage conversation about the video topic and join in!
